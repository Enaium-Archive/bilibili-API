## 用户信息

https://api.bilibili.com/x/space/acc/info?mid=用户UID

|名字|说明|
|----|----|
|mid|UID|
|name|名字|
|sex|性别|
|face|头像|
|sign|简介|
|birthday|生日|
|fans_badge|是否开通粉丝徽章|
|top_photo|主页图片|

|名字|说明|
|----|----|
|mid|UID|
|following|关注数|
|follower|粉丝数|
